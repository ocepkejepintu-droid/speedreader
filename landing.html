What it does

●

Focused reading

One word anchored on the same focal point, with the red letter as the optical recognition point. Your eyes never move. Choose your WPM; pause on punctuation.

🔒

Your library stays yours

Files live on your device. Progress syncs across phone, tablet, and desktop through iCloud Drive. No account, no telemetry, no subscription.

📱

Works everywhere

It's a PWA. Install on iOS, Android, macOS, Windows, or Linux. Same URL everywhere. Same library. Same chapter, same line.

How it works

1

Drop in an EPUB

Drag a file onto the library. It stays on your device, no upload, no conversion step.

2

Pick a mode

RSVP for speed, Phantom for context, or Scroll for a normal read. WPM is a slider, not a setting you commit to.

3

Open it anywhere

Same URL on your phone and your library is there. Pick up at the last word, not the last chapter.

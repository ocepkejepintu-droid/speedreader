Send to RSVP

Right-click the button and "Bookmark this link" works on most browsers. On Safari, drag it to the bookmarks bar.

What it does

Strips scripts, navigation, headers, footers, asides, and forms. Trims to 50,000 characters. Sends the title, the URL, and the cleaned text to the RSVP Reader share endpoint. The reader opens with the article loaded in your library.

Privacy

The request goes to zipang.id (where this PWA is hosted). The bookmarklet does not call any other domain.
